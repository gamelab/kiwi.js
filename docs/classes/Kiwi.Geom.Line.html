Kiwi.Geom.Line Class

Defined in: src\geom\Line.ts:9
Module: Geom
Parent Module: Kiwi

A Kiwi Line object has two meanings depending on the situation you need. Either an infinte line through space (this is the normal meaning of a Line) OR it can be a Line Segment which just exists between the TWO points you specify.
